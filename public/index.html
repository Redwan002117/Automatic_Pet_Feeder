How It Works

Getting started with your Automatic Pet Feeder is easy and intuitive.

1

Set Up Your Device

Connect your feeder to Wi-Fi and pair it with your account through our mobile app.

2

Create Feeding Schedules

3

Fill the Feeder

4

Monitor & Control

Use the app to track feedings, receive notifications, and adjust settings as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Automatic Pet Feeder.

Our feeder is designed to work with most dry pet foods, including kibble and treats. The portion sizes are adjustable to accommodate different food shapes and sizes. Wet food is not recommended for use with the automatic feeder.

Setting up feeding schedules is easy through our mobile app. You can establish multiple feeding times throughout the day, specify portion sizes, and even set up different schedules for different days of the week.

Our feeders come with a built-in battery backup that will keep the unit operational during power outages for up to 48 hours, ensuring your pet never misses a meal. The system will also notify you when it's operating on battery power.

Yes, you can use one feeder for multiple pets that eat the same food. However, for pets with different dietary needs, we recommend using separate feeders. For selective feeding, consider our premium model with microchip recognition technology.

Our standard model holds up to 6 pounds of dry food, which typically lasts 2-3 weeks for an average-sized cat or small to medium dog. Our large capacity model holds up to 12 pounds, suitable for longer periods or larger pets.
