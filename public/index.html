Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PetFeeder

How does the automatic feeding work?

PetFeeder uses a programmable timer and a mechanical dispensing system to release a pre-set amount of food at scheduled times. You can program these times and portion sizes through our mobile app.

What type of food can I use with PetFeeder?

PetFeeder works best with dry kibble between 1/8" and 3/4" in size. It's not designed for wet food, fresh food, or treats that might clog the dispensing mechanism.

What happens if my Wi-Fi goes down?

PetFeeder has an offline mode that will continue to dispense food according to the last saved schedule, even if your Wi-Fi is unavailable. Once your connection is restored, the device will automatically reconnect and sync with the app.

How big is the food container?

The standard PetFeeder holds 6 pounds (about 25 cups) of dry kibble. The PetFeeder XL, designed for multi-pet households, can hold up to 12 pounds (about 50 cups).

Can I control multiple PetFeeders from one app?

Yes! You can control as many PetFeeder devices as you need from a single app. Each device can have its own custom schedule, portion sizes, and settings.
