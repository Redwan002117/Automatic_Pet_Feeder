Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our automatic pet feeder

How does the automatic pet feeder work?

Our automatic pet feeder uses a programmable timing mechanism connected to a food dispensing system. You schedule feeding times through our app, and the feeder dispenses the pre-set amount of food at those times. The device connects to your home Wi-Fi, allowing you to control and monitor it remotely via our mobile application.

What kind of pet food can be used with the feeder?

Our feeder works best with dry pet food (kibble) between 0.2-0.6 inches (0.5-1.5 cm) in size. It's not suitable for wet food, fresh food, or very large kibble pieces. The food container is designed to keep food fresh and prevent jamming.

What happens if my internet connection goes down?

The feeder will continue to work based on your last programmed schedule, even without an internet connection. While you won't be able to control it remotely or receive notifications during an outage, the scheduled feedings will continue uninterrupted.

How accurate are the portion sizes?

Our feeder uses a precision dispensing mechanism that's accurate to within ±5% of the selected portion size. You can calibrate the portion sizes in the app based on your specific pet food to ensure the most accurate measurements.

Is there a warranty on the pet feeder?

Yes, all our feeders come with a 1-year standard warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Premium and Family plans include an extended 2-year warranty. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied with your purchase.

How long does the battery backup last?

The battery backup can power the feeder for up to 48 hours during a power outage, depending on usage. The app will notify you when the feeder switches to battery power and when the battery level is getting low.
